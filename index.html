Tariff News Impact Dashboard

Max Maksutova, Mollie Schuma, Nicole Sellers, Sarah Wozniak · Team 8 · Spring 2025

This dashboard visualizes how stock prices respond to tariff-related news events. It displays the last 30 days of stock data with vertical news lines indicating when events occurred. If a single stock is selected, hovering over a news line will show the news details and its impact (percentage change from the previous day).
