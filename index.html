Tariff News Impact Dashboard

Max Maksutova, Mollie Schuma, Nicole Sellers, Sarah Wozniak · Team 8 · Spring 2025

This dashboard visualizes how stock prices respond to trade and tariff-related news events. It shows the last 30 days of stock data with local news events overlapped on the chart. When you select a single stock, the chart also calculates the impact (percentage change from the previous day) on news days.
