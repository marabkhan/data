Tariff News Impact Dashboard

Max Maksutova, Mollie Schuma, Nicole Sellers, Sarah Wozniak · Team 8 · Spring 2025

This dashboard visualizes how stock prices respond to trade and tariff-related news events. By tracking key companies and overlaying relevant headlines, it helps reveal potential correlations between policy announcements and market movements. Use the dropdown to select a stock and view recent price trends alongside major news headlines.
