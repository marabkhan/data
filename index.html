Tariff News Impact Dashboard

Max Maksutova, Mollie Schuma, Nicole Sellers, Sarah Wozniak · Team 8 · Spring 2025

This dashboard visualizes how stock prices respond to trade and tariff-related news events. It shows the last 30 days of stock data with local news events overlapped as vertical lines. When a single stock is selected, hover over a news line to view the news title, description, and the impact (percentage change from the previous day).
